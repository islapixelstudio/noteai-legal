Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data (Account / Contact)

If you create an account or contact support, we may collect information such as:

  • Email address (if you register or contact support)
  • User/account identifier (internal ID used to keep your account signed in)

User Content

The Service is designed to process user content. Depending on which features you use, this can include:

  • Audio recordings you upload or record in the app (for transcription)
  • Transcripts generated from audio
  • Text you input (e.g., notes, prompts, or content used to generate summaries)
  • Imported content identifiers (e.g., a YouTube link you provide) when you request a transcript

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. It may include device identifiers, operating system version, IP address, timestamps, crash logs, and diagnostic events used for troubleshooting and improving reliability.

AI Processing & Consent (Third-Party AI Services)

NoteAI uses third-party AI service providers to deliver certain features such as speech-to-text transcription and AI-generated summaries.

What data is sent for AI processing

  • Transcription: audio recordings (and related metadata needed to complete transcription)
  • Summaries / custom summaries: transcript text and/or text you provide for summarization

Who the data is sent to

Depending on the feature used, we may send data to the following providers:

  • AssemblyAI (speech-to-text transcription)
  • OpenAI (text generation / summarization)

Permission (consent)

Before the Service sends your content to third-party AI providers, we present an in-app disclosure and request your permission. If you do not consent, AI processing features will not be used.

Purpose limitation

We send your content to AI providers only to perform the requested feature (e.g., create a transcript or summary) and return results to the app. We do not sell your user content.
